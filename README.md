# Day3_Tensor-Operations
Performed basic tensor operations such as addition and multiplication to understand computational flow in TensorFlow.

Here is the detailed explanation for Day 3 of your TensorFlow internship project:

The focus shifted from understanding tensors to performing operations on them. Since neural networks rely heavily on mathematical computations, it was important to understand how TensorFlow performs operations such as addition, multiplication, and other arithmetic functions.

Basic tensor operations were implemented using functions like tf.add() and tf.multiply(). These operations demonstrated how TensorFlow executes mathematical computations efficiently. The concept of element-wise operations was also explored, where corresponding elements of tensors are processed together.

Additionally, this day helped in understanding how TensorFlow builds computational graphs behind the scenes. Even though eager execution allows immediate results, internally TensorFlow organizes operations in a structured computation flow for optimization.

By the end of Day 3, a strong conceptual understanding was developed about how data flows through mathematical operations in TensorFlow. This formed the foundation for understanding neural network calculations such as weighted sums, activation functions, and loss computation in the upcoming days.
